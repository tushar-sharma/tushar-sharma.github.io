Anna Karenina Leo Tolstoy

It's a journey into the heart's chaos. Witness Anna's captivating allure and downfall, alongside Levin's profound quest for life's meaning. A story rich in beauty, tragedy, and insight.
Great Expectation Charles Dickens

Pip's life flips with one kind gesture to a fugitive, spiraling him into a world of heartache, mystery, and love with Miss Havisham and Estella. It's a story of how a single act of kindness can shape one's destiny.
Man's Search for Meaning Viktor Frankl
{% for i in (1..5) %} {% endfor %}
Frankl's memoir is a profound odyssey through history's darkness, revealing life in concentration camps and the quest for meaning amidst despair. A riveting, inspirational read.
Wings of Fire: An Autobiography A. P. J. Abdul Kalam
{% for i in (1..3) %} {% endfor %}
It is more than an autobiography; it's the inspiring journey of India's beloved President and the 'Missile Man', A.P.J. Abdul Kalam. From humble origins to pioneering India's space and missile programs, his life story ignites the dreams within us all.
The Five People You Meet in Heaven Mitch Albom

A poignant journey through heaven's lessons, where one man's life is beautifully unraveled through the five people he meets. A tale of understanding life's intricate connections and the impact of every encounter.
The Selfish Gene Richard Dawkins
{% for i in (1..5) %} {% endfor %}
Journey with Dawkins as he unravels the compelling saga of genes and survival. A concise, profound exploration that shifts the paradigm of evolution.
