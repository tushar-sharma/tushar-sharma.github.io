Epitaph of Cashew:

> Fat as a piggy,  
> yet fast like thunder,  
> he squeaked for carrots,  
> and munched on hay like a snack.  
>   
> He liked to cuddle,  
> obedient as a cow,  
> never made a mess,  
> was cute until he crossed the rainbow bridge
