## Langauge Devil May Cry 5

Change language in `Devil May Cry 5`. Go to the installation folder. Find the file `steam_emu.ini` and update the langauge

```
###                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       ### Language that will be used in the game                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                ###                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Language=english   
```
