## U.S. Immigration

USCIS Director Joseph B. Edlow tweeted[^edlow]

> At the direction of @POTUS, I have directed a full scale, rigorous reexamination of every Green Card for every alien from every country of concern.
 
Green card application vetting have become more stringent under Trump's second term. USCIS had earlier said that it would now interview neighbors, coworkers, or employers of naturalization applicants to confirm eligibility for U.S. citizenship[^uscis].
