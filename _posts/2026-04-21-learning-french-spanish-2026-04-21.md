My rough notes while learning French and spanish.


| English             | French              | Pronunciation (French)                  | Spanish         |
| ------------------- | ------------------- | --------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Hi                  | Bonjour             | **bohn-zhoor**                          | Hola            |
| you                 | vous / tu           | **voo / too**                           | tú              |
| where               | où                  | **oo**                                  | dónde           |
| where are you from? | D’où venez-vous ?   | **doo vuh-nay voo**                     | ¿De dónde eres? |
| I am from India     | Je viens d’Inde     | **zhuh vyan dاند** (≈ *zhuh vyan dund*) | Soy de India    |
| My name is Tushar   | Je m’appelle Tushar | **zhuh mah-pell too-shar**              | Me llamo Tushar |
|                     | Moi, c’est Tushar   | **mwah say too-shar**                   |                 |
| Nice to meet you    | Enchanté(e)         | **ahn-shahn-tay**                       | Mucho gusto     |
| How are you?        | Comment ça va ?     | **koh-mahn sah vah**                    | ¿Cómo estás?    |
| I am fine           | Ça va bien          | **sah vah byan**                        | Estoy bien      |
| I love you          | Je t’aime           | **zhuh tem**                            | Te amo          |
| but                 | Mais                | **meh** (not “may”)                     | pero            |
