## Meaningless Rambling 

I have found myself short of compfort lately. The rustle of leaves arouses my curiosity no more. Or the melting sundown fails to draw my attention. It has dawn to me that I have forsaken my past self; an adamant fool bumbling with hope & ravaging lofty ideas cornerning the world. 

It was not long ago, if I can recollet before my age beging to seep in. My slumber have put lead on my eyes leaving my fragile fingers at the mercy of the keyboard. 

This is a meaningless rambling. But it has some meaning burired inside which I at present dont' want to unearth. The shoot of memories have becomre rigid to hamper my attempt to excavate my memories. Where are they? Are they gone? 

Not yet. Not yet. It is meaningless rambling..
